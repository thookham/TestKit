## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of TestKit seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please follow these steps:

1.  **Do not open a public issue.**
2.  Email the details of the vulnerability to [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS].
3.  Include as much information as possible to help us reproduce the issue.

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and provide an estimated timeline for a fix.
